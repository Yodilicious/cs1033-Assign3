Stellar Stationery launched in 2010 by creating one-of-a-kind, handmade wedding and event invitations. The business has expanded to include a wholesale division and Stellar Stationery introduced their first "imprintable" invitation with a custom look. This filled a void in the wedding industry by offering a much-needed alternative to the traditional engraved invitation. The consumer was now able to purchase a box of invitations from a local store, print them at home and have the result be as elegant as a custom designed product. Their signature vellum and ribbon, combined with her use of antique engravings, botanicals and fabrics, separated their products from the competition.
